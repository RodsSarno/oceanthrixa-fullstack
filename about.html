Our story

Oceanthrixa was born from the collaboration of electronic engineers and oceanographers with a single goal: to create reliable equipment for underwater environments. From lab prototypes to commercial solutions, durability and integration are our priorities.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

Deliver secure, affordable subsea electronic solutions that enable data collection and automation in marine environments.

Vision

Become a regional reference for subsea technology, innovating in sensors and water-resistant connectivity.

Values

  • Reliability
  • Transparency
  • Responsible innovation
  • Sustainability
